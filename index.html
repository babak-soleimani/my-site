MUSIC WRITING MAPS

I explore the overlap of music, architecture, and spatial analysis. My work traces how cultural spaces shape listening, gathering, and presence.

I perform with AgawDilim, write about design histories of interiors, and develop geographic visualizations using open cultural datasets.

Current research: mapping the shift from intimate, shadowed "nook" spaces to the contemporary "airspace" aesthetic in cafés and studios after 2010.
